**Rapide555** is a board of my //RapideXXX// series, small boards for quick prototyping, with everything you need around one component. This time, the all famous [[timers and 555|555]]!

Presentation

Principle

Small board, monopurposed or multipurposed if better. For me 555 rhymes with PWM, so that will be the base business case (<- I'm working too much). Then around that I want to be able to hijack that quickly into other fun use cases of the mythic 555: monostable, on-off button etc... I won't fit them all, there's entire books about 555 circuits! Just make sure I have enough pins free for soldering the puppy in other config than PWM.

Points of interrest

Implementation

Bill of materials

* 555 in DIP format (I like it retro, and 8 pins anyway you're not saving much with the SMD version) * PPTC? * Power regulation? * 2 free PCB holes per pin, in case * Solder switch ready to close to immediately get a PWM config * Optional status LED

Schematics

PWM555 (as in [[Aquarium Lights]])


Gives a nice 98% - 8% or less range. Could be made better though. * IC1: 555 in DIP format * R1 and R2: 100 ohm * R3: 470 ohm * D1: Schottky diode * C1: 0.1 uF * Q1: IRFZ44N MOSFET * R6: potentiometer, 1k - 10k
