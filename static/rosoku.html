A hot and cold candle, the Rosoku

Ok, so Rosoku (that should be written "rousoku" or even better 蝋燭　ろうそく) means candle in japanese. I stumbled upon that project today (2012/02/08) and found it quite interresting, like the kind of thing you can do on a big evening or a sunday afternoon, to change your mind of other long run projects. Also looks perfect to finally play with thoses Attiny13 I bought the other day, I have at least one spare LM35 for the temperature, a few RGB leds, some button battery ... hum...
It is also my first project using AVR Studio 5, which is a great tool but missing a little something...
