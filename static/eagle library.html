What's inside ?

As of 2012/02/08:
  • DS3231 : I found something in another .lbr file but it was not my format so I imported the existing and adapted to my needs
    • SO16 : the small SMT package
    • SO16W : the wide one that is in fact the size of the DS3231 I had
  • 2 way solder pad : bridge to left or right. This exist also in the Sparkfun.lbr, I just noticed too late.
  • RGB SMT led : some led I found at Akizukidenshi, roughly 6mm x 6.6mm, 6 pins. no way I spend hours doing acrobatic soldering with that. Lets make a nice landing and solder it by hand but on a nice board.
