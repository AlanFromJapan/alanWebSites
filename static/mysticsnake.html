**MysticSnake** is an experiment, proof of concept/framework to develop RPG like game. Not meant to be a do it all, but reusable for any top view simple level scrollers. And it's an infinite project since I code at night when kids let me have free time, sometimes with MONTHS of pauses...

Presentation

Principle

* Uses fixed map (no infinite scroller) but handles any size up to 32x32 * Internal generators for maps * Basic AI * Zelda like movements

Points of interrest

* Well it's a gameboy game, so it says everything or nothing to you
